Features &
Benefits

Review Site Posting

You get customer feedback first, then with a single click, request
them to post to your favorite Review Site, including Google, Yelp,
Facebook.

Review Site Monitoring

Your ReviewKeep Portal shows you all reviews posted to sites like
Google, Yelp and Facebook and allows you to directly respond to any
postings.

Location and Subsidiaries

Have multiple locations, or even organizations with different brand
names? No issue, keep track of all locations and different
organizations, or roll up all stats for an enterprise-wide overview.
Compare and contrast locations to see who is performing best.

Reporting

The ReviewKeep Platform has a myriad of reports, all geared
towards giving you the best insight possible on your organization
and your competitors!

Competitor Monitoring

Want to see how that competitor is down the street, regionally, or
nationally? No problem, put them in your competitor list and
ReviewKeep monitors their profiles and give you insightful reporting
so you can keep your competitive advantage!

Reminders

ReviewKeep automatically sends out reminders on industry standard
increments. However, you can send reminders to complete review
requests and surveys at your preferred increments in the manual
settings tab.

Customer Portal

ReviewKeep is a mini CRM allowing you to enter all pertinent
customer data including contact information, notes, birthdays, and
ReviewKeep monitors their profiles and give you insightful reporting
preferences.

Marketing

Customers like to know you care. Want to send customers birthday or holiday cards? No problem! Want to send out a coupon to drive business? No problem! Experiencing a slower than normal period and want to get customer attention, send out a discount card good for the next few days! Increase touch points to keep your business the first consumers think when repurchasing your products and services!
