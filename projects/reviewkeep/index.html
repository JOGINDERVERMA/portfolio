Gain

Reviews are the best way to build company and product/service credibility. A recent study by Trust Working Group has shown that trust in advertising has reached a record low of 25%. People are turning to consumer reviews to research and confirm company, product, and service credibility. Reviews are critical to your company credibility, branding, and reputation.

Increase
Revenue

On average, reviews produce an 18% uplift in sales, significantly more for those with high volumes of positive reviews. By managing your reviews with the ReviewKeep Platform, you can proactively gain positive reviews, while identifying potential poor reviews and handling them before they post to a public site.

Grow
Customers

86% of consumers hesitate to do business with a company if it has a large percentage of negative reviews. 88% of consumers put as much weight on reviews as personal recommendations, with some studies stating they are more important. With a properly managed review system, you can gain customers by ensuring your review score is high.

Issues

Utilizing the ReviewKeep Platform, you can easily identify customer issues including issues that are trends and one-offs that may be due to a bad day by an employee, or a bad batch of product in the supply chain.
